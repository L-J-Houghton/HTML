Personal Statement

I am an individual with a hard work ethic , a well rounded skillset and a wide range of enterprising skills. I have previously worked in the IT industry and would like to prosper and pursue within the industry I have a high amount of passion for. I have always had a passion for technology and since the age of 14 I have taught myself several programming languages through looking at online material and reading books and have developed simple programs that I have used to test my programming and coding skills.


I have also worked with 3d visual graphics and have created 3d models and short basic test animations within Autodesk Maya and 3ds max. I was inspired by Architecture of buildings and designed and rendered my own concepts within multiple 3d design programs. I then used these 3d models along with my coding and programming skills to develop short demo games within the Unreal development kit (UDK). This then inspired me to look into the app development market and particulary the gaming app market. In which I had researched and constructed analytical data in order to inspire ideas for future apps , websites and software.


I have an even logical and creative balance and therefore I can easily imagine and envision a project or task aswell as using the logical and practical thinking on order to achieve the vision or task at hand. I have taken a particular liking to business within the tech industry over the years and would like to build my own company from the ground up. I always aim for something out of my comfort zone as it pushes me to succeed more knowing it is a challenge and therefore I give even more dedication and comittment to the project or task that I am working on.


Overall I believe that whatever you're doing that you love isn't really work , it's just fun and something that brings you joy and fulfilment.
